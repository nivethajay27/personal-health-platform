# Insight Engine

## Decision

Start with rule-based insights that combine cycle, food, workout, recovery, energy, mood, and symptom data.

## Why This Matters

Insights make the app feel useful instead of becoming a collection of logs. They show how the app turns daily tracking into patterns users can understand.

## First Insight Types

- Energy trends by cycle phase
- Luteal cravings and nutrition patterns
- Menstrual fatigue and recovery needs
- Sleep changes before period start
- Workout intensity recommendations by phase
- Symptom patterns across cycle days

## Language Rules

Use careful pattern-based language:

- "Your logs show..."
- "This appears more often..."
- "This may be related to..."

Avoid medical certainty:

- "This caused..."
- "This diagnoses..."
- "You should treat..."

## Tradeoff

Rule-based insights are less advanced than AI-generated recommendations, but they are easier to control, explain, test, and keep privacy-friendly.
